Kitsune

Here at Kitsune, we believe learning is a lifetime endeavour. Kitsune includes lessons on using advanced search operators to return the best results for a user's search. Our site includes a Google custom search engine featuring open source encyclopedias, eBooks and textbooks for those who wish to learn about any subject at no cost. We also provide a YouTube search engine on our site to explore subjects as videos that can be saved to your Kitsune blog. Our goal is to provide a safe space for users of any age or background to explore interesting topics and subjects at their own pace.
